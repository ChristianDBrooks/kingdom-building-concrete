WHAT WE DO

Services

At Steezy we craft enterprise level sites and put them in the hands of small business owners all over the United States. We build static sites with 100% hand-written code and styles resulting in high performance. With higher performance, comes more customers, which means more money in your pocket.

Hand Coded Websites

Every line of code is carefully written by hand. Which produces a lightweight website. Wordpress and Wix sites are filled with unnecessary code and styles because a program is trying to do a programmer’s job. Google detects this extra bulk and ranks sites lower that are not efficient. Static websites will beat almost any Wordpress site.

Static Websites

Website load times matter. 40% of consumers will wait no more than three seconds before abandoning a site. This goes up to 74% when waiting five seconds on mobile. With our hand-coded static website your business website load times will be lightning fast.

Responsive Web Development

60% of web traffic happens on mobile phones. That means you need a site that looks and feels good on mobile. We got you covered.

Custom Web Design

We create personalized websites that are tailored exactly to what you need. Everything on your site should feel and function the way you want. 64% of customers with a bad experience on a website will purchase from a different store next time.
